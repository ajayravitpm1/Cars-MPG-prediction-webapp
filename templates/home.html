Miles per Gallon Prediction

Miles per Gallon of a car depends upon its Horse power (HP),Volume (VOL),Speed (SP) and Weight (WT). This is a web application that predicts Miles per Gallon of a car when given its values of Horse power in hp, Volume in litres, Speed in miles per hour and Weight in kilograms using Machine Learning based on that linear relationship between MPG and Horse power, Volume, Speed and Weight.
